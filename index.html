About Forensic Integrity Group

Forensic Integrity Group specializes in the forensic review and legal analysis of wiretap recordings. We support attorneys, law firms, investigators, and government agencies by transforming complex surveillance data into clear, accurate, and defensible intelligence.

Our Foundation

Built on more than 35 years of combined experience across military intelligence, law enforcement, legal practice, and education, our firm brings a disciplined, multidisciplinary approach to sensitive investigative and legal matters.

Our Focus

We concentrate exclusively on the intersection of surveillance, compliance, and evidentiary integrity — ensuring wiretap operations, documentation, and findings withstand legal scrutiny at every stage.

What Sets Us Apart

Our Founders

Scott Woodlee

Principal Partner & Senior Investigator

Scott Woodlee is the founder and Senior Investigator of Forensic Integrity Group, a specialized firm focused on the review and analysis of wiretap recordings for legal and investigative purposes.

With over 15 years in law enforcement, including 8 years in narcotics conducting wiretap investigations, Scott has participated in more than 30 state and federal wiretap cases. He also conducted the first heroin wiretap investigation in Central Florida, earning recognition for precision, discretion, and investigative insight.

In addition to his law enforcement career, Scott served 20 years as a U.S. Army paratrooper specializing in strategic, tactical, and special operations intelligence. This dual background allows him to provide trusted oversight on sensitive surveillance operations, ensuring all intelligence gathered is actionable and legally sound.

Stephanie Groulx

Principal Partner, Legal & Analytical Services

Stephanie Groulx is the co-founder of Forensic Integrity Group and brings a diverse, global perspective to the firm. She holds a law degree from Université du Québec à Montréal (Faculty of Political Science and Law) and practiced as a legal counselor for seven years advising small and medium-sized businesses.

After earning her MBA, Stephanie spent over 15 years as a school teacher and media specialist, combining analytical thinking with strong organizational and communication skills. Fluent in both English and French, she adds valuable legal and linguistic expertise to the company’s operations.

Together, Scott and Stephanie lead a firm committed to delivering accurate, confidential, and high-quality wiretap review services tailored to legal and investigative professionals.
